Our Mission

Blue Ivy Speech and Debate is an initiative aimed at increasing educational access to speech and debate resources.

Our organization aspires to provide guidance and teaching for more students who have a passion for speech and debate. We also aim to make Blue Ivy Speech and Debate more widespread to open more opportunities to other students all around the country.

Impact

  • Reaching over 185+ students across the United States
  • Curriculum is all original work by BISD coaches
  • All classes are provided at no-cost, hosted digitally, and available to anyone in the US in order to be accessible

What We Offer

Programs

Since 2022, Blue Ivy Speech and Debate has empowered students through summer camps and free virtual workshops. Led by experienced debaters, our programs range from week-long debate intensives to webinars focused on fundamental skills and debater insights. Students engage in topic lectures, case writing, and mock tournaments, gaining the confidence and competitive edge to succeed in debate and beyond.

Lori Li
President, Blue Ivy Speech & Debate

Lori has done 4 years of debate in Lincoln-Douglas, Public Forum, and Parliamentary Debate. While mainly a traditional Lincoln-Douglas Debater, she started circuit debating in the 25-26 season and was the champion of the UCLA Invitational, went 5-1 at the National Speech and Debate Season Opener, and is this year's Intermediate LD New York State Champion.

Katherine Sheng
Vice President, Blue Ivy Speech & Debate

With three years of competitive experience, Katherine has a strong foundation in both Public Forum debate and Speech. She most notably reached the Octofinals at Lakeland and finished 1st and 3rd in the Potomac Intramurals.
